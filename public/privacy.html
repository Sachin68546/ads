Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 18, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how our application ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our app and log in through Facebook OAuth to access your Meta Ads account data.

1. Information We Collect

When you log in using Facebook OAuth, we collect the following data (with your permission):

  • Public profile information (name, email address)
  • Facebook User ID
  • Ads account data, including:
    • Ad account ID and name
    • Ad campaigns, ads sets, and ads details
    • Performance metrics (impressions, clicks, etc.)

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Authenticate your identity via Facebook OAuth
  • Access and display your Meta Ads account data securely within our app
  • Provide analytics or dashboard services based on your ads performance
  • Improve our app’s functionality and performance

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to others. We may share data only when:

  • Required by law or legal process
  • Necessary to protect our legal rights or comply with legal obligations
  • With your explicit consent

4. Data Security

We take appropriate security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. This includes:

  • OAuth 2.0 secure login with Facebook
  • Encrypted storage and secure servers
  • Access control and regular security reviews

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data from our system
  • Withdraw access to your Meta data by revoking app permissions in your Facebook settings

6. Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with the Meta (Facebook) platform. Their use of your data is governed by their own privacy policy, which you can review at:

https://www.facebook.com/privacy/policy

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the “Effective Date” at the top of this page.
